We’re hiring AI-native Gen-Z gaming specialists

Genhood is a small, fast studio building social casual games for the creator generation. We hire people who live in games, creator culture and AI — and use AI in their craft every day, not as a buzzword.

Influence Manager / SMM Specialist (AI-integrated)

● Open Marketing · Growth Remote-friendly English Full-time

You’ll own how Genhood and Bloghouse Rivals show up across social — and how we partner with real creators — with AI woven through your entire workflow, from ideation to production to analysis. This is a hands-on role for someone who ships content daily and treats AI tools as a force multiplier.

What you’ll do

  • Run our social presence across TikTok, Instagram/Reels, YouTube Shorts and X — strategy, calendar, and daily posting in a native Gen-Z voice.
  • Produce short-form content at studio speed using AI for scripting, image/video generation, editing and variation — turning game moments into scroll-stopping clips.
  • Build and manage influencer collaborations, including revenue-share creator partnerships, from outreach to briefing to performance.
  • Drive organic growth and community — trends, formats, comment culture, UGC loops that bring players in at low cost.
  • Measure everything — track reach, engagement, install/UA-adjacent signals, and feed insights back into content and product.
  • Prototype with AI constantly — find, test and standardize the tools and prompt workflows that make a one-person content team punch like ten.

You probably are

  • Fluent in Gen-Z internet and creator culture — you feel trends before they peak.
  • AI-native: LLMs and image/video generators are already part of your daily process, and you have opinions about which ones win.
  • A strong content sense with a portfolio of social posts, short-form video, or channels you’ve grown.
  • Comfortable owning outcomes in a small team with a lot of autonomy.
  • Excellent written English; additional languages are a plus.

Nice to have

  • Experience marketing a mobile game or a creator/influencer brand.
  • An existing network of creators or influencers.
  • Hands-on with paid UA, ASO, or community/Discord building.

Why Genhood

  • Get in early at an AI-native studio (founded Nov 2025) with a live first title.
  • Real ownership and a direct line to the founders — your work ships immediately.
  • Build the playbook for AI-powered creator marketing from scratch.

About the studio

Bridging casual gaming and the creator economy

Our mission: empower the next generation of creators to turn their passion into a career — through games they can’t put down.

Genhood (Gengames Interactive) is an AI-native games studio founded in November 2025. We started building from day one, and our first title — Bloghouse Rivals — is already live on Google Play.

We make social casual mobile experiences that turn the creator fantasy into play, then pair it with real AI tools so players don’t just dream about digital stardom — they practice it. The next wave of creative stars is already playing; we hand them a stage.
